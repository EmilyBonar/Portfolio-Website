Sinter Cracking Experiments

• Researched sintering and crack growth of ceramics to prevent sinter cracking in manufacturing • Designed samples with Solidworks and used SLA and binder jet 3D printers to create them • Developed process to analyze thousands of photos using ImageJ to measure crack length and relative density • Co-authored “In situ observations of cracking in constrained sintering” in the Journal of the American Ceramic Society, which earned a “Best Paper of 2018” award from the journal
